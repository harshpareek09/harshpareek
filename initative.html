Our Initatives

Activity 1

We conduct awareness programs on a daily basis in schools and colleges, telling students that their safety is in their hands. If they see any violence, they should inform the police or their parents and not get involved in any kind of abuse. They must raise their voice against any kind of misbehavior that happens to them.

Activity 2

We consistently write emails and use social media to protest against corruption and bribery. We also organized a protest named "Na rishwat denge na lene denge," which went pretty well, and we got things done eventually.

Activity 3

We monitor every project passed by the government to ensure the work is done properly. For example, we check the materials used for road construction in our area to ensure they are working properly and not keeping people in the dark.
